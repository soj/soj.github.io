# Finance Tracker

**Stack:** SwiftUI · SwiftData · Swift Charts · CloudKit
**Link:** [github.com/your-handle/finance-tracker](https://github.com/your-handle/finance-tracker)

An app for tracking personal income and expenses. Categories, budgets, clear
charts built with Swift Charts, and sync across devices via iCloud.

Storage is built on SwiftData, analytics on Swift Charts. It supports recurring
transactions, CSV export and quick entry through shortcuts.
