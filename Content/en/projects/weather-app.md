# Weather

**Stack:** Swift · SwiftUI · WeatherKit · WidgetKit
**Link:** [github.com/your-handle/weather-app](https://github.com/your-handle/weather-app)

A weather forecast app for iPhone. Hourly and weekly forecasts, home-screen
widgets and animated backgrounds that change with the weather and time of day.

Data comes from WeatherKit, the UI is entirely SwiftUI, and widgets use
WidgetKit. Special care went into smooth transitions and offline support.
