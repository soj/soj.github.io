# Hi! This is my blog

Here I'll share notes on developing for Apple platforms: interesting problems
from Swift and SwiftUI, approaches to app architecture, and my own projects.

This site is built with [Publish](https://github.com/JohnSundell/Publish) —
a static site generator written in Swift by John Sundell. It's convenient when
both your site and your apps are written in the same language.

Check out the [portfolio](/en/projects/) to see what I've worked on.
